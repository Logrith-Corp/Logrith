The short version: Logrith does not collect, store, or process your files. Everything runs in your browser. We collect minimal anonymous analytics. We don't sell any data. Ever.

1 Data We Do NOT Collect

Logrith is architecturally designed to never see your data. The following is never collected, transmitted, or stored:

  • Your files — images, PDFs, documents processed through any Logrith tool
  • Your passwords — generated by Password Generator or Password Strength tool
  • Your search queries — typed into any Logrith search field
  • Your text inputs — entered into word counters, JSON formatters, or any other tool
  • Account information — because no accounts exist on Logrith

2 Analytics Data We Collect

We collect minimal, anonymous analytics data to improve the service:

  • Page views and which tools are used (no file contents)
  • General geographic location (country/region level only)
  • Browser type and device type (for compatibility testing)
  • Referral source (how users find Logrith)

This data is aggregated, anonymous, and cannot be linked to any individual user. We do not use it for advertising targeting or sell it to any third party.

3 Cookies

Logrith uses minimal cookies:

  • Essential cookies: For basic site functionality (language preferences, theme settings)
  • Analytics cookies: Anonymous usage tracking only (no personal data)

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting the core functionality of any Logrith tool.

4 Third-Party Libraries

Logrith loads some JavaScript libraries from CDN servers (Cloudflare CDN) for PDF-Lib, PDF.js, and QRCode.js. These CDN loads may record your IP address in standard server logs as part of normal CDN operation. No file content is ever transmitted — only the library code is fetched.

5 Advertising

Logrith may display advertisements to support the free service. Advertising providers may use cookies to serve relevant ads. These are separate from Logrith's own data practices. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your browser's privacy settings or ad industry opt-out tools.

6 Children's Privacy

Logrith does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age. Our tools do not require any personal information to use. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us at privacy@logrith.in.

7 Data Security

Since Logrith does not collect or store personal data or file contents, there is no meaningful data breach risk for user files. The website is served over HTTPS to protect page content in transit. Our minimal analytics data is stored with industry-standard security practices.

8 Your Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and Indian data protection law, you have rights including access, correction, deletion, and portability of personal data. Since we do not collect personal data beyond minimal anonymous analytics, these rights have limited practical application. For any privacy concerns, contact privacy@logrith.in.

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when we make changes to the platform. Material changes will be indicated by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We will not reduce your privacy rights without explicit notice.

10 Contact for Privacy

For privacy-related questions or concerns, contact us at privacy@logrith.in or through our Contact page.
