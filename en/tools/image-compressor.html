Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I reduce image file size?
Typical compression at 75% quality reduces JPG/WEBP files by 40–80% with minimal visible quality difference. PNG compression is lossless and depends on image complexity.
Will compressing reduce image quality?
At 70–80% quality, the difference is barely visible to the human eye. For web use, 75–80% is the sweet spot between size and quality. Use the quality slider to find your balance.
Can I compress multiple images at once?
Yes! Upload up to 10 images at once. All will be compressed with the same settings and available to download individually or all at once.
Is WEBP better than JPG for compression?
Yes. WEBP typically produces 25–34% smaller files than JPG at the same quality. It's the recommended format for modern websites.

Why Compress Images for Your Website?

Large images are one of the biggest reasons websites load slowly. Studies show that 53% of mobile users abandon sites that take over 3 seconds to load. Compressing images can dramatically improve page load speed, SEO rankings, and user experience.

Logrith's image compressor uses the HTML5 Canvas API to re-encode your images at the specified quality level — all in your browser. No third-party servers, no privacy concerns, no file size limits beyond what your device can handle.
