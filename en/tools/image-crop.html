Frequently Asked Questions

How do I crop an image online?
Upload your image, then drag on the preview to select the crop area. You can also type exact pixel coordinates in the X, Y, Width, Height fields. Click Crop Image to apply.
Can I crop to a specific aspect ratio?
Yes! Use the preset buttons (1:1, 16:9, 4:3, 9:16) to automatically set a crop area with the correct proportions centered on the image.
Will cropping affect image quality?
No, cropping only removes the parts outside your selection. The remaining area retains its full original quality.
