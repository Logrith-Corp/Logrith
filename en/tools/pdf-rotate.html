Frequently Asked Questions

How do I rotate a PDF page?
Upload your PDF, select the rotation angle (90°, 180° or 270°), choose whether to apply to all pages or specific ones, then click Rotate PDF. Download the result instantly.
Can I rotate only certain pages?
Yes! Select "Specific Page Range" and enter pages like "1-3, 5". Or choose "Odd Pages Only" or "Even Pages Only" for quick batch selection.
Will rotating reduce PDF quality?
No. PDF-Lib rotates pages by modifying page metadata — no re-encoding occurs. The content quality is perfectly preserved.
