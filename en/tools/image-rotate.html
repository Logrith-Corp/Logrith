Frequently Asked Questions

How do I rotate an image 90 degrees?
Upload your image, then click "Rotate 90° Right" or "Rotate 90° Left". The preview updates instantly. Apply as many rotations as needed then download.
What's the difference between flip and rotate?
Rotating turns the image clockwise or counterclockwise. Flipping mirrors it — Flip Horizontal mirrors left-to-right (like seeing it in a mirror), Flip Vertical mirrors top-to-bottom.
Can I combine rotate and flip?
Yes! You can apply multiple transforms. The preview updates after each operation. Click Reset All to undo all transforms and start fresh.
