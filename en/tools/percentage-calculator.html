Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate 20% of 500?
Use the "What is X% of Y?" calculator. Enter 20 as the percentage and 500 as the number. Result: 20% × 500 = 100.
How do I find percentage increase?
Use the "Percentage Change" calculator. Enter the old value and new value. Formula: ((New - Old) / |Old|) × 100. A positive result = increase, negative = decrease.
