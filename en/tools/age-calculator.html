Frequently Asked Questions

What is an age calculator?
An age calculator is an online tool that helps you calculate your exact age from your date of birth. It can show your age in years, months, days, hours, and minutes in a simple and accurate format.
How is age calculated exactly?
Age is calculated by measuring the difference between your date of birth and the selected calculation date. The calculator considers month lengths and leap years to give a more accurate result.
Can I calculate age for a past or future date?
Yes. Use the "Calculate Age As Of" field to find age on a past date or estimate age on a future date based on the selected value.
Can I check days left until my next birthday?

How to Calculate Age Manually

You can calculate age manually by subtracting your birth year from the current year. After that, compare the current month and day with your birth month and day. If your birthday has not happened yet this year, subtract one year from the total.

To calculate exact age in months and days, you must also account for the number of days in each month and special cases like leap years. This is why an online age calculator is more convenient, especially when you need a quick and accurate answer.
