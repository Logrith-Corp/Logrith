Frequently Asked Questions

How is age calculated exactly?
Age is calculated by finding the difference between your date of birth and today's date (or a specified date). The tool accounts for leap years and varying month lengths to give you the precise number of years, months, and days.
Can I calculate age for a past or future date?
Yes! Use the "Calculate Age As Of" field to set any date — past or future. This lets you find someone's age on a specific historical date or calculate future age.
Why does age differ by timezone?
This calculator uses your device's local timezone. If your birth time is near midnight at a timezone boundary, the exact day count might differ by 1 from calculations done in a different timezone.
