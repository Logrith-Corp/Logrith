Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need a character counter for social media?
Twitter/X limits 280 characters, Instagram captions 2200, LinkedIn posts 3000, SMS 160 chars per message. Exceeding limits truncates your content or splits messages.
Should I count characters with or without spaces?
Most platforms count WITH spaces (Twitter, SEO meta). Use "Total Chars" for platform limits, "No Spaces" for keyword density analysis.
What is character frequency analysis used for?
Cryptography (frequency attacks), language detection, SEO keyword optimization, text compression, detecting unusual character patterns in data.
Does this counter work for essays and assignments?
Yes! Get exact word count, character count (with/without spaces), reading time estimates for academic writing requirements.
