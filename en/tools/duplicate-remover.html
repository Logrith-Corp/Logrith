Frequently Asked Questions

How do I remove duplicate lines?
Paste your text in the input box and click Remove Duplicates. The tool keeps only the first occurrence of each line and removes all subsequent duplicates.
Can I sort lines alphabetically while removing duplicates?
Yes! Check "Sort A→Z" or "Sort Z→A" before clicking Remove Duplicates. The output will be both deduplicated and sorted.
