Frequently Asked Questions

How do I resize an image to specific dimensions?
Upload your image, enter the desired width and height in pixels, then click Resize. If aspect ratio lock is on, changing one dimension auto-adjusts the other to maintain proportions.
What does "Lock Aspect Ratio" mean?
Aspect ratio is the proportional relationship between width and height. When locked, if you change width, height auto-adjusts so the image doesn't look stretched or squished.
What are good image sizes for websites?
Hero images: 1920×1080. Blog images: 1200×800. Thumbnails: 300×200 or 400×400. Social media OG images: 1200×630. Profile pictures: 400×400.
Will resizing reduce file size?
Yes — reducing pixel dimensions directly reduces file size since there are fewer pixels to store. A 1920×1080 image resized to 800×450 will be significantly smaller.
