Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert meters to feet?
Select "Length" category, enter your value, choose "Meter" as the from unit, and click Convert. The result will show feet, inches, yards, kilometers, miles and all other length units simultaneously.
How is temperature conversion different from other units?
Temperature uses offset formulas rather than simple multiplication. Celsius to Fahrenheit: (°C × 9/5) + 32. Celsius to Kelvin: °C + 273.15. The tool handles these automatically.
What data size units are supported?
The tool supports bits, bytes, kilobytes (KB), megabytes (MB), gigabytes (GB), terabytes (TB), kibibytes (KiB), mebibytes (MiB), gibibytes (GiB) — using both SI (1000-based) and binary (1024-based) standards.
