Frequently Asked Questions

How do I extract specific pages from a PDF?
Upload your PDF, then enter the page range (e.g., "1-5, 8, 10-12") and click Split. The tool extracts those exact pages into a new PDF file.
Can I split a PDF into individual pages?
Yes! Select the "Split All Pages" tab and click Split. Each page will be available as a separate downloadable PDF.
Will the split PDFs maintain original quality?
Absolutely. The split uses PDF-Lib to extract pages without any re-encoding, preserving the original quality and formatting.
