The Complete Browser-Based Tool Platform

Logrith brings together a growing collection of fast, browser-based utilities designed for developers, creators, students, businesses, and everyday users. Instead of switching between multiple websites for PDF editing, image conversion, text formatting, calculations, and developer utilities, everything is organized in one clean and consistent platform.

Every tool on Logrith is designed for speed, simplicity, and privacy. Most tools process data directly inside your browser without unnecessary uploads or complicated setup. Whether you are converting images, merging PDF files, formatting JSON, generating QR codes, calculating GST, resizing images, or checking password strength, the experience stays fast, lightweight, and mobile-friendly.

The platform currently includes categories such as Calculate & Convert Tools, Text & Generator Tools, Developer Utilities, Image Tools, PDF Tools, and everyday productivity utilities. New tools and workflows are continuously added to expand the ecosystem while keeping the interface simple and easy to navigate.

Logrith focuses on practical tools people actually use daily — file conversion, formatting, optimization, calculations, content utilities, security helpers, and browser-based productivity workflows — all without requiring downloads, installations, or account creation.
