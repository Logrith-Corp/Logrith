Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Overview

This policy explains what information MINRAL collects when you browse the store and how that information is used. MINRAL does not process payments directly — all purchases are completed through external checkout providers such as Gumroad or similar platforms, which maintain their own privacy policies.

Information we collect

  • Basic usage data such as pages visited, collected through standard analytics
  • Information you voluntarily submit through the contact form (name, email, message)
  • No payment or card information is collected or stored by MINRAL

How checkout data is handled

When you click a Buy Now or Get Instant Access button, you are redirected to a third-party checkout provider. Any information you provide there — including payment details and email — is governed by that provider's own privacy policy, not this one.

Cookies

MINRAL may use minimal cookies for basic site functionality and analytics. No cookies are used for third-party advertising.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through the Contact page or to support@logrith.in.
