A small, deliberate catalog.

What MINRAL is, why it exists, and what we actually sell.

What MINRAL is

MINRAL is a premium storefront for digital products — playbooks, templates, prompt systems, and startup kits built for people who build things for a living. Every product is created to be used immediately, not read once and forgotten.

Why it exists

Most digital product marketplaces are built around volume — thousands of low-effort templates competing for attention. MINRAL takes the opposite approach: a small catalog, priced fairly, maintained over time, with nothing published that we wouldn't use ourselves.

What we sell

Our catalog spans AI workflows, business operating systems, startup kits, productivity systems, templates, prompt packs, and long-form guides — organized so you can find exactly what solves the problem in front of you, without wading through filler.

Our positioning

Premium doesn't mean expensive here — it means considered. Clean design, clear writing, instant delivery, and products that hold up after the first use. That's the whole brand.
