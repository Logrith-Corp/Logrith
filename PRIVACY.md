# Privacy Policy - Logrith

**Last Updated:** May 24, 2026

At **Logrith** ([logrith.in](https://logrith.in)), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our platform.

## How Logrith Works

### Frontend-Only Architecture

Logrith is built entirely with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Here's what happens:

1. **You visit logrith.in** → HTML/CSS/JS files are downloaded to your browser
2. **You use a tool** → Your browser processes everything locally
3. **No servers involved** → No data transmission beyond the initial page load

### What We Process

- **Images:** Converted, compressed, resized entirely in your browser's Canvas API
- **PDFs:** Processed using browser APIs - never uploaded
- **Text:** Formatted, encrypted, analyzed - all in your browser
- **Files:** Handled by browser's File API - no server upload

### What We Don't Process

❌ No data is sent to external servers  
❌ No file uploads beyond your browser  
❌ No user tracking or profiling  
❌ No behavioral analytics  
❌ No personal information collection

## Cookies and Tracking

### Our Cookie Policy

**Logrith does NOT use tracking cookies**

The only cookies that may be set are:

1. **Session Cookies** (if needed)
   - Technical operation only
   - Deleted when you close browser
   - No tracking purpose

2. **Local Storage** (Optional)
   - User preferences (stored locally)
   - Tool settings
   - Your choice to use
   - Can be cleared anytime

### How to Disable Cookies

If any cookies are set, you can disable them:
- **Chrome:** Settings → Privacy → Cookies
- **Firefox:** Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
- **Safari:** Preferences → Privacy
- **Edge:** Settings → Privacy
