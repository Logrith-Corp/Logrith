About Logrith Calculate & Convert Tools

Logrith Calculate & Convert Tools are designed to make everyday calculations, conversions, and quick utility tasks simple, fast, and accessible from any device. Whether you need to convert units, calculate percentages, estimate EMI payments, calculate GST, or work with color formats, every tool is built for instant usage without unnecessary complexity or account creation.

The Unit Converter supports a wide range of measurement categories including length, weight, temperature, speed, area, volume, pressure, and digital storage units. It is useful for students, developers, engineers, businesses, and everyday users who need quick and accurate conversions while working, studying, shopping, or traveling.

The Percentage Calculator helps calculate percentages, increases, decreases, discounts, profit margins, and percentage differences instantly. It can be used for shopping discounts, exam marks, financial calculations, business analysis, and day-to-day calculations without manually solving formulas.

For financial calculations, the EMI Calculator provides quick monthly installment estimates for loans, including home loans, car loans, personal loans, and education loans. Users can adjust loan amount, interest rate, and tenure to compare repayment options and better understand total interest costs over time.

The GST Calculator simplifies tax calculations for Indian users by supporting GST-inclusive and GST-exclusive calculations across different tax slabs. It helps businesses, freelancers, shop owners, and consumers quickly calculate final amounts, tax values, and billing estimates.

Designers and developers can also use the Color Converter to switch between HEX, RGB, and HSL color formats instantly. This is especially useful for web design, frontend development, graphic design, branding work, and UI creation where different platforms require different color formats.

Every tool on Logrith is optimized for speed, mobile responsiveness, and simplicity. No installation or technical setup is required — simply open the tool, enter your values, and get accurate results instantly in your browser.
