What is EMI?

EMI stands for Equated Monthly Installment - the fixed amount paid monthly toward loan repayment including principal and interest.

EMI Formula

EMI = [P × R × (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N - 1]

  • P = Principal loan amount
  • R = Monthly interest rate
  • N = Number of months

Loan Types

  • Home loans/Mortgages
  • Car/Vehicle loans
  • Personal loans
  • Education loans
  • Business loans
