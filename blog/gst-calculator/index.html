What is GST?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is a consumption tax applied to goods and services. It's calculated as a percentage of the product/service value.

Common GST Rates

  • 5% - Essential goods
  • 12% - General goods
  • 18% - Standard rate
  • 28% - Luxury items

GST Calculation

Total with GST = Price + (Price × GST%)
