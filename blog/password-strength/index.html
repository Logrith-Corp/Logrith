Password Strength Levels

  • Weak: Easy to crack, vulnerable
  • Fair: Moderate protection
  • Good: Reasonably secure
  • Strong: Highly secure
  • Very Strong: Maximum protection

Strength Factors

  • Password length
  • Character variety
  • No dictionary words
  • No personal information
  • Special characters
